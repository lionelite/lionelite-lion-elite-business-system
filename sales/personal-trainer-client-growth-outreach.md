## Bluesky Outreach Strategy

### Target Audience

Prioritize public Bluesky posts from:

- Independent personal trainers
- Online fitness coaches
- Strength coaches
- Body transformation coaches
- Small gym owners
- Boutique fitness owners
- Trainers building an online coaching offer
- Trainers trying to grow their personal brand

### High-Intent Signals

Prioritize posts containing intent such as:

- Need more clients
- Looking for clients
- Trying to grow my coaching business
- How do trainers get clients
- Need help marketing my training
- Starting online coaching
- Struggling to get leads
- Need more personal training clients
- How do I scale personal training
- Want to grow my fitness business
- Need better client retention
- Clients are not staying consistent
- How do I get better transformations
- Building my fitness brand
- Trying to grow on social media
- Need help selling coaching

### Lead Scoring

Score public posts from 0–100.

**90–100 — Immediate Opportunity**
Trainer explicitly says they need clients, leads, sales, marketing help, or help scaling their coaching business.

**75–89 — Strong Opportunity**
Trainer is launching coaching, discussing low client volume, struggling with retention, or actively asking growth questions.

**60–74 — Nurture Opportunity**
Trainer discusses business growth, transformations, content, accountability, or building their brand without explicitly asking for help.

**Below 60 — Monitor**
General fitness conversation without meaningful business or buying intent.

### Categorization

Tag matching prospects with:

- `personal-training`
- `trainer-client-acquisition`
- `fitness-business-scaling`
- `online-coaching`
- `client-transformation`
- `trainer-personal-brand`

### Public Reply Framework

Do not immediately hard-sell every prospect.

Use a value-first response that connects directly to the problem expressed in the post.

Example:

> One of the biggest shifts for trainers is turning transformations into the acquisition system itself. Better client experience → better results → stronger proof → easier client acquisition. We help trainers build that entire flywheel.

### Conversation Opener

When a prospect engages:

> We help personal trainers get more clients while building systems that improve the transformations of the clients they already have. The goal is to make your results become your marketing engine instead of constantly chasing the next client.

### Discovery Questions

Ask:

1. How many active clients are you working with right now?
2. How many new clients would you ideally add each month?
3. Where are most of your clients currently coming from?
4. What happens after someone shows interest in coaching with you?
5. What is currently stopping you from scaling?
6. How are you documenting and marketing your client transformations?
7. What would your business look like if you had a predictable client acquisition system?

### Close

> If we could help you bring in more personal training clients while simultaneously helping your existing clients achieve transformations that make your business easier to sell, how valuable would that be to you?

## Automation Rules

The Bluesky listener should continuously identify public trainer posts showing business-growth intent, categorize them, score them, and place the strongest opportunities at the top of the Lion Elite Intelligence pipeline.

Priority order:

1. Explicit need for clients or leads.
2. Explicit request for marketing/sales help.
3. Starting or scaling online coaching.
4. Client retention/accountability problems.
5. Desire for stronger client transformations.
6. Personal-brand/content growth.
7. General fitness-business discussion.

Store the public post URL, handle, post text, detected niche, intent category, score, reason for score, and recommended outreach angle.

Respect platform rules, public-data boundaries, opt-outs, and do-not-contact status. Do not fabricate contact information or infer private contact details.
