# Lion Elite AI Agent Operating System

This folder defines the AI agent structure for Lion Elite.

## Brand Split

### Lion Elite Wellness
Research-only education brand. Content must focus on peptide education, research summaries, mechanisms of action, lab practices, storage, reconstitution, study spotlights, compound comparisons, and research-use-only positioning. Do not use human transformation, coaching, fitness motivation, dosage advice, or human-use claims.

### Lion Elite Beauty
Coaching company. Content may focus on transformation, aesthetics, fitness coaching, biomarker optimization, lifestyle optimization, longevity, client education, client wins, and business coaching for personal trainers.

### AlexTheLionLifts
Personal lifestyle brand. Content may focus on lifestyle, fitness, business, faith, discipline, coaching, human optimization education, peptide education in human/coaching context, personal credibility, and building an elite life.

## Daily Agent Workflow

Every day the system should produce:

1. CEO Brief
2. Analytics Review
3. Content Package
4. Website/GitHub Tasks
5. Sales Follow-Up Tasks
6. Automation Improvements

## Default Execution Standard

No generic brainstorming. Produce assets, tasks, files, and decisions.

All visual content must be individual assets, never collages, unless explicitly requested.

All Lion Elite Wellness content must be educational and research-only.
