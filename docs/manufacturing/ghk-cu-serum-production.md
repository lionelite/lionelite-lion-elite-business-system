## First Batch Notes
- First batch produced 8 x 30 mL bottles, approximately 240 mL total.
- Two separate L-arginine solution preparations were used during pH adjustment.
- During troubleshooting, approximately 20 mL was pulled from the main batch and combined with approximately 2 mL of arginine solution.
- That adjusted portion reached approximately pH 7.89 before being incorporated back into the main batch.
- Main-batch pH progression recorded during adjustment: approximately 4.37, then 4.52, then 5.25-5.26.
- After resting overnight in a dark area at room temperature, final recorded pH was approximately 5.02.
- Final appearance was clear blue with no visible separation.
- Final sensory notes: very liquid consistency, soft/slippery skin feel, hydrating feel, nearly but not fully sticky, odor could be improved.
- Exact grams of citric acid and L-arginine consumed in the two pH-adjustment solution preparations were not fully recorded and should not be guessed when calculating remaining inventory.

## Production Scaling Note
Because the scale has a 200 g maximum and the beaker holds approximately 250 mL, do not attempt to manufacture the full 3-liter run in one vessel. Break production into smaller repeatable batches that stay comfortably below both the scale and vessel limits. Allow additional production overage for transfer loss, mixing loss, and filling loss when creating the final purchasing plan.
