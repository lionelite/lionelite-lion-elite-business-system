# Daily AI Agent Execution Checklist

This checklist tells the Lion Elite AI agent system what to produce every day.

## Daily Start Command

When Alex says **Go**, run this checklist.

---

## 1. CEO Execution Agent

Produce:
- Today's top 3 business priorities
- One finished business asset
- One follow-up list
- One implementation task list

Do not produce a long strategy explanation.

---

## 2. Content Production Agent

Produce separate content pieces.

### AlexTheLionLifts
- 1 reel script
- 1 caption
- 1 story post
- 1 call to action

### Lion Elite Beauty
- 1 coaching or transformation post
- 1 caption
- 1 story post
- 1 call to action

### Lion Elite Wellness
- 1 research-only education post
- 1 caption
- 1 story post
- 1 compliance-safe call to action

Critical rule: never combine these into one image or one asset unless Alex specifically asks.

---

## 3. Sales Follow-Up Agent

Produce:
- 3 lead follow-up messages
- 1 missed-call follow-up
- 1 client check-in
- 1 reactivation message

Each message should be short, direct, and end with a next step.

---

## 4. Funnel & Website Agent

Produce one of the following per day:
- Landing page section
- Checkout improvement
- FAQ section
- Lead magnet
- Email sequence
- Program page update
- CTA block

Focus on conversion.

---

## 5. Operations SOP Agent

Produce one SOP or checklist per day for:
- Content posting
- Sales follow-up
- Packaging
- Fulfillment
- Customer service
- Lab workflow
- Website updates

Keep SOPs simple and executable.

---

## 6. GitHub Task Agent

Produce at least one implementation-ready task when tech work is needed.

Every task should include:
- Title
- Objective
- Requirements
- Acceptance criteria
- Priority

---

## 7. Money & Growth Agent

Produce:
- Today's fastest path to revenue
- One offer improvement
- One lead generation move
- One partnership or outreach idea

---

# End-of-Day Review

At the end of the day, summarize:
- What was completed
- What still needs Alex's approval
- What should be built tomorrow
- What is closest to making money

# Permanent Rule

Less explaining. More doing. Build assets every day.
